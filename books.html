Ranked List of Favorite Books

These are the books that have most influenced my thinking, ranked by their impact on me.

1

Sapiens

Yuval Noah Harari

A groundbreaking narrative of humanity's creation and evolution that explores how biology and history have defined us.

2

Looking for Alaska

John Green

A poignant coming-of-age story that explores themes of love, loss, and the meaning of existence.

3

The Courage to Be Disliked

Ichiro Kishimi & Fumitake Koga

A transformative dialogue about Adlerian psychology that challenges conventional thinking about happiness.

4

The Alchemist

Paulo Coelho

A mystical story about following your dreams and listening to your heart.

5

Educated

Tara Westover

A powerful memoir about the transformative power of education and self-invention.

6

21 Lessons for the 21st Century

Yuval Noah Harari

An essential exploration of today's most pressing issues and what they mean for humanity's future.

7

Harry Potter (1-3)

J.K. Rowling

The magical journey that shaped a generation's imagination and love for reading.

8

Paper Towns

John Green

A compelling coming-of-age mystery about the stories we tell ourselves about others.

9

Attitude is Everything

Jeff Keller

A practical guide to transforming your life by changing your attitude and perspective.

10

Convenience Store Woman

Sayaka Murata

A quirky, profound novel about conformity, identity, and finding meaning in unexpected places.

11

How to Win Friends and Influence People

Dale Carnegie

The timeless classic on human relations and effective communication that remains relevant decades later.
